Welcome to Blood Group Identification Page!


The ABO Blood Group System includes four major blood groups and eight distinct blood types. These groups are defined by the presence or absence of two specific antigens and antibodies - A and B :

  • Group A - Antigen A and Antibody B.
  • Group B - Antigen B and Antibody A.
  • Group AB - Antigen A and B, no Antibodies.
  • Group O - No Antigens, both A and B Antibodies.
Rh factor:
  • Agglutination with anti-D serum indicates Rh-positive (e.g., A+).
  • No agglutination indicates Rh-negative (e.g., A-).
